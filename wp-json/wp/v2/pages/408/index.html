Dentistry is the medical field focused on oral health. Dentists diagnose, treat, and prevent dental issues, from routine cleanings to complex procedures like oral surgery and orthodontics. They also emphasize the importance of good oral hygiene and educate patients about maintaining healthy teeth and gums. Dentistry includes various specialties, such as orthodontics, periodontics, and cosmetic dentistry, each addressing specific aspects of oral health and aesthetics. Overall, dentistry is essential for both oral well-being and overall health.<\/p>\n<\/div>
